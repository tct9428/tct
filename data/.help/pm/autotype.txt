⌨️ *AutoType (Ghost Writer) Manual*

This module simulates human typing presence (showing "typing..." or "recording...") in chats. It makes the bot appear more active and human-like.

_Note: The bot does not actually send a message; it just shows the typing status._

✦═════════◆═════════✦

*1. Enable or Disable*
Turn typing simulation on or off for the current chat (or target).

`.type on`
> • _Explanation:_ Enables auto-typing with default duration.

`.type off`
> • _Explanation:_ Disables auto-typing.

✦═════════◆═════════✦

*2. Setting Duration*
Control how long the bot shows "typing..." before pausing. Use a number from 1 to 12.
_(Each unit is approx 5 seconds. So 12 = 60 seconds.)_

`.type <number>`

> • *Example:* `.type 6`
> • _Explanation:_ The bot will type for about 30 seconds (6 * 5s) whenever it detects activity.

✦═════════◆═════════✦

*3. Global Mode (Owner Only)*
Decide where the bot is allowed to type globally.

`.typemode <mode>`

> • *Modes:*
> `g` = Groups Only (Default)
> `p` = PMs Only
> `all` = Both Groups and PMs

> • *Example:* `.typemode all`
> • _Explanation:_ Allows the bot to simulate typing in both private chats and groups.

✦═════════◆═════════✦

*4. Managing Other Groups (Remote)*
Configure settings for other groups using extensions.

`.type <command> jd:groupName`

> • *Example:* `.type 12 jd:Support`
> • _Explanation:_ Sets the "Support" group to have long typing durations (60s).
> • *Example:* `.type off jd:all`
> • _Explanation:_ Disables auto-type in *all* groups.
