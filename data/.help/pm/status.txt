📥 *Status Saver Manual*

This module automatically downloads *every* status update posted by your contacts and forwards it directly to your private chat (or the Owner's chat) the moment it is posted.

_Note: Unlike "Anti-Status" (which recovers deleted ones), this module saves everything immediately._

✦═════════◆═════════✦

*1. Enable or Disable*
Turn the auto-saver on or off.

`.status on`
> • _Explanation:_ The bot will start forwarding every new status update to your private chat.

`.status off`
> • _Explanation:_ Stops the auto-forwarding.

✦═════════◆═════════✦

*2. Checking Status*
See if the saver is currently active.

`.status`

> • _Output:_ Shows `🟢 ON` or `🔴 OFF`.
