📖 *Auto-Read Manual*

This module automatically marks every incoming message as "Read" (Blue Ticks) instantly. This is useful for keeping your unread counter at zero or letting people know you are online.

_Note: This is a global setting. It affects private chats and groups._

✦═════════◆═════════✦

*1. Enable or Disable*
Turn the auto-read system on or off.

`.read on`
> • _Explanation:_ The bot will instantly blue-tick every new message it receives.

`.read off`
> • _Explanation:_ The bot will stop marking messages as read automatically.

✦═════════◆═════════✦

*2. Status Check*
Check if the module is currently active.

`.read`

> • _Output:_ Shows `🟢 ON` or `🔴 OFF`.

✦═════════◆═════════✦
