*1. Setting a PM Filter*
Create a trigger and a response.
You must also set a **Priority** (`?pr`) to tell the bot which filter is more important if keywords overlap.

`.pflt <trigger>`
`?pr <priority>`
`! <response>`

> â€¢ *Example 1 (Basic):*
> `.pflt hi, hello`
> `?pr 1`
> `! Hello! I am a bot. How can I help?`
> â€¢ _Explanation:_ If anyone DMs you "hi", the bot replies automatically.

> â€¢ *Example 2 (Business):*
> `.pflt price, cost, rates`
> `?pr 10`
> `! Our services start at $50/hour.`
> â€¢ _Explanation:_ Sets a high priority response for pricing inquiries.

*Why is `?pr` (Priority) important?*
The `?pr` number determines the "weight" of the rule.
If a user's message matches multiple filters, the bot will choose the one with the **highest priority number**.
*Always include it to ensure your most important rules trigger first.*
