*1. Setup: Importing Cookies (Required)*
Before you can download, you must load your yt cookies.
_This is required to bypass age restrictions and bot detection._

*Step A: Get Cookies*
1. Install a "Get cookies.txt LOCALLY" extension on your browser.
2. Go to yt and make sure you are logged in.
3. Export cookies in **Netscape HTTP Cookie File** format.

*Step B: Load into Bot*
You can paste the text directly or reply to a file.

`.setcookies <content>`
