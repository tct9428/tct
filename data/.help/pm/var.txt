*Command Prefixes:*

*2. Alive Images (Config.yml)*
Manage the random images sent by the `.alive` command.

*Replace List:*
`.var alive: <url1>, <url2>`
> â€¢ _Explanation:_ Deletes the old list and replaces it with these new URLs.

*Add to List:*
`.var aliveadd: <url>`
> â€¢ _Explanation:_ Keeps existing images and adds new ones to the rotation.

*Remove from List:*
`.var alivedel: <url>`
> â€¢ _Explanation:_ Removes a specific URL from the list.
