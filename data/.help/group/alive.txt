⚡ *Alive & Uptime Manual*

This module checks if the bot is online, displays how long server has been running (uptime), and sends a daily motivational quote.

_Note: The alive message is sent as a "Card" with a random image._

⊷═══════════════════⊷

*1. Check Status*
Send the alive message immediately to see if the bot is responding.

`.alive`

> • _Explanation:_ The bot replies with a card containing a random image, a "Quote of the Day", and the current system uptime.

•═══════════════════•

*2. Daily Schedule*
Configure the bot to automatically send the alive message every day at a specific time.

`.alivedaily on <HH:mm>`

> • *Example:* `.alivedaily on 08:00`
> • _Explanation:_ The bot will send the quote and uptime every day at 8:00 AM.

`.alivedaily off`
> • _Explanation:_ Disables the daily schedule.

⊷═══════════════════⊷

*3. Custom Message (Footer)*
You can add a custom note that appears at the bottom of the alive card (under the uptime).

`.setalive <text>`

> • *Example:* `.setalive Maintained by @Admin`
> • _Explanation:_ Adds "Maintained by @Admin" to the bottom of the alive message.

`.delalive`
> • _Explanation:_ Removes the custom message.

•═══════════════════•

*4. Managing Other Groups (Remote)*
Check status or configure schedules for other groups using extensions.

`.alive jd:groupName`

> • *Example:* `.alive jd:all`
> • _Explanation:_ Sends the alive message to *all* groups in your JIDBook.
> • *Example:* `.alivedaily on 07:00 jd:WorkGroup`
> • _Explanation:_ Sets the daily schedule for "WorkGroup" to 7:00 AM.
