> â€¢ *Example:* `.gstsched add 08:00 Good Morning`

> â€¢ *Example:* `.gstsched in 1 h Meeting starting soon`
> â€¢ _Explanation:_ Sends the status exactly 1 hour from now, then deletes the schedule.
