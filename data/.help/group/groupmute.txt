This module allows you to automatically close (mute) and open (unmute) your group at specific times.
*New in V2:* You can now schedule different times for specific days (e.g., Weekends vs Weekdays) or set "Close Only" / "Open Only" tasks.

*1. Setting a Schedule*
The new format requires you to specify the *Days* first.

`.gm add <days> <CloseTime>|<OpenTime> <Message?>`

*Supported Days:*
`all`, `daily`, `weekdays`, `weekends`, or specific days like `mon,wed,fri`.

> â€¢ *Example (Daily):*
> `.gm add all 23:00|07:00 Sleep Time`
> _Explanation:_ Closes at 11 PM and opens at 7 AM every day.

> â€¢ *Example (Weekends):*
> `.gm add weekends 00:00|09:00 Weekend Rest`
> _Explanation:_ Closes at midnight and opens at 9 AM on Sat/Sun only.

*2. Single Actions (Close or Open Only)*
You don't have to set both times. You can set a rule just to close or just to open.

*Close Only:*
`.gm add <days> close <HH:mm> <Message?>`
> â€¢ *Example:* `.gm add fri close 18:00 Happy Weekend`
> _Explanation:_ Closes the group every Friday at 6 PM.

*Open Only:*
`.gm add <days> open <HH:mm> <Message?>`
> â€¢ *Example:* `.gm add mon open 08:00 Work starts`
> _Explanation:_ Opens the group every Monday at 8 AM.

*3. Managing Schedules*
Since you can have multiple schedules, use IDs to manage them.

*View Active Schedules:*
`.gm list`
> _Explanation:_ Shows all rules with their ID numbers, days, and times.

*Delete a Schedule:*
`.gm del <ID>`
> â€¢ *Example:* `.gm del 2`
> _Explanation:_ Deletes the specific rule with ID 2.

*Clear All:*
`.gm clear`
> _Explanation:_ Wipes all schedules for the current group.
