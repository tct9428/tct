> â€¢ *Example:* `.spm window 5:2`

`.spm burst <count>`
> â€¢ *Example:* `.spm burst 10`
> â€¢ _Explanation:_ Instantly triggers if 10 messages arrive at once.

*3. Punishment Modes*
Decide what the bot should do when it catches a spammer.

`.spm mode warn`
> â€¢ _Explanation:_ The bot will only reply telling the user to stop. It will not kick them.

`.spm mode kick`
> â€¢ _Explanation:_ The bot will warn the user first. If they continue to spam (usually 3 times), the bot will *Kick* them out of the group.
